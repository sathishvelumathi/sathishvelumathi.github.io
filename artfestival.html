Art Festival

The art festival is a yearly festival that VIT - School of architecture hosts. Artists from all over India send their paintings and works to VIT, Vellore and put up in display in the institution. Artists drop by to conduct free workshops for students of the institution. Workshops like etching, clay moulding, pottery and live sketching are conducted.
