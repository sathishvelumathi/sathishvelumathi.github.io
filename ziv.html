Auditorium

A Redevelopment proposal for an existing auditorium was floated to Estancia Vidya Mandir school, Guduvanchery, Chennai. The Existing Auditorium is just a stage and an empty site in front of it for gathering.
The design proposal was to use the existing stage structure and build a new auditorium lifted from the ground. This way the lower portion can be used as a multipurpose shaded area as before and the upper portion as a 600 seater capacity auditorium.

Estancia Township, Guduvanchery, Chennai | Ar. Sriram Ganapathy
